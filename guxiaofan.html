Take Care of Your Worries

Gu Xiao Fan

In 2015 summer, I designed an APP for a company.

 

The APP provieds service for who is seeking for psychological counseling.

 

People can use it to chat with psychological Consultants with a little fee every month.

 

I joined the team as a UXUI desinger .The daily work is designing new screens and redesign the workflow and wireframes.

 

I enjoyed the work and published the first vision after 2 months.

 

Now they have a online website and an new vision APP.

In 2016, I try to change its original main color into a pink green completely.

 

The new style get rid of the childish feeling but retains the feeling of cute and sunny.

After discuss the product's features and needs with PM, I draw the wire frames and work flow.

 

It's helpful for communication to draw some sketches when we are puzzled.

You can see my workflow in MockingBot.
